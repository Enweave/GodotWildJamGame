About:

The General and the Snow Fox is a story-driven game that takes place on Penumbra, a tidally locked planet resulting in extreme weather and terrain where few can survive.

This unforgiving world is home to the Shapesettlers, a shapeshifting people who have been gifted with abnormally long lives. Having emerged from caves to establish settlements above ground, they were often victim to the deadly vermin and predators that survived within the Withered lands, a frozen forest at the edge of their home.

Over centuries lived behind their great walls, the tales of these deadly beasts faded to myth; stories told around the light of pyres during the darkest hours. It was only when the General’s son snuck beyond the wall and was taken by a Snow Fox were they reminded that the stories were true.

Now the General must brave the Withered Lands to track the Snow Fox and retrieve his son.

Controls:
Left mouse button Melee attack
Right mouse button Ranged attack
WASD Move
Space Dash
E Interact
